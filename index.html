About

Fei-Fei Li is a Chinese-American computer scientist, author, and AI researcher. She is widely recognized as one of the most influential figures in Artificial Intelligence, specializing in computer vision, machine learning, and cognitive neuroscience. She advocates for human-centered AI and inclusive technology that benefits society.

Fun Fact: she also served as Chief Scientist of AI/ML at Google Cloud and is the director of the Stanford Artificial Intelligence Laboratory from 2013 to 2018.

ImageNet Project

Fei-Fei Li led the ImageNet Project, creating a large-scale visual database that trained deep learning algorithms to recognize images. This project revolutionized computer vision globally.
